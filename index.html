The Basics

I’m kinda a country hick who appreciates the finer or better things in life. My life travels and experiences have exposed me to a variety of people and geographies. These experiences led me to feel at home in the small rural parts of the west.

Real and down to earth people are important to me. I’m very tired of “fake” folks who are trying to be something or someone just to impress someone or to feel self-important.

Roots

I grew up in a small farm town in Eastern Idaho, St Anthony. Following my ninth grade year, we moved briefly to Orange County California. This lasted only 8 months, eventually retreating back to Eastern Idaho to the town of Idaho Falls. I attended college in North Idaho, University of Idaho in Moscow. I’ve spent many summers in Sun Valley Idaho and 20 years in Seattle before moving back to Idaho. I now reside in Boise on the western side of Idaho.

Dreaming and/or reliving the past

I’m way beyond a dream job at this stage in my life. I’m thinking more about retirement than working. But, reality gets in the way and I still have to work for a few more years. I did a part time gig with them and MLB.com a few years back supporting the pitch tracking project that is deployed in each MLB ball park. If I didn’t mind living in big cities, I would have continued with this endeavor. It was a killa great time.
